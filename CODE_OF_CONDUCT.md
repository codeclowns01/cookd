# Code of Conduct

## The Short Version

Be direct. Be specific. Don't waste people's time.

cookd is built by developers for developers. The community standard is professional — not warm and fuzzy, not hostile. You are expected to disagree technically and move on. Personal attacks, harassment, and bad-faith engagement are grounds for removal.

## What's Expected

- Criticism is about code and design, not people
- Bug reports and PRs contain enough detail to be actionable
- Disagreements are resolved by reasoning, not volume
- New contributors are treated as competent until proven otherwise

## What's Not Tolerated

- Harassment, personal attacks, or targeted hostility
- Dismissing contributors based on anything other than the quality of their work
- Bad-faith issue reports intended to waste maintainer time

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to [info@codeclowns.com](mailto:info@codeclowns.com). Reports are handled privately. Confirmed violations result in removal from the project at maintainer discretion.
